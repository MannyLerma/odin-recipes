Krabby Patty

For when you can't decide if you want spaghetti or tacos - have both!

The popular meal of Spencer, from the hit show "iCarly".

Get the best of both worlds and be the envy among your friends and family.

Ingredients

  • hamburger buns with sesame seeds
  • all-beef hamburger patties
  • cheddar cheese slices
  • red onion
  • medium dill pickle
  • large tomatoes
  • romaine lettuce
  • mustard
  • ketchup

Recipe instructions

  1. Grill patties until they are almost cooked through.
  2. Add the cheese on top and cook until melted.
  3. Spread the mustard and ketchup on the buns. Put the patties on top of each.
  4. Add the lettuce, tomatoes, pickles, and onion. Top with the top bun
