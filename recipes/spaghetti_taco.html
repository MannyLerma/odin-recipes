Spaghetti Tacos

For when you can't decide if you want spaghetti or tacos - have both!

The popular meal of Spencer, from the hit show "iCarly".

Get the best of both worlds and be the envy among your friends and family.

Ingredients

  • ground beef or ground turkey
  • packet of taco seasoning
  • a cup of water
  • a jar of spaghetti sauce
  • a box of spaghetti
  • hard taco shells
  • Salsa (optional)

Recipe instructions

  1. Prepare noodles according to package. Drain and return pot.
  2. Brown meat in pan. Add taco seasoning and water. Bring to a boil. Then reduce heat and simmer for 5-6 minutes until sauce thickens. Add spaghetti sauce and cook until warmed. Add noodles and mix thoroughly.
  3. To assemble the tacos, place a spoonful of salsa in the shell. Sprinkle with a small amount of cheese. Finally, finish filling the shell with spaghetti. Add lettuce, if desired.
