Milk Steak

Enjoy this delicious entree for you and the gang!

Do you enjoy watching "It's Always Sunny in Philadelphia"? Well you'll enjoy this!

Charlie Kelly's favorite meal, with a side of jelly beans, first seen in the episode "The Waitress is Getting Married".

Ingredients

  • 1 medium flat iron steak
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp nutmeg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup jelly beans

Recipe instructions

  1. Add the milk, honey, cinnamon, nutmeg, and vanilla to a medium sized saucepan. Then heat up the mixture, stirring until the honey has been completely dissolved.
  2. After bringing the mixture to a boil. Carefully place the steak in the boiling milk mixture. Bring the liquid back to a boil and reduce the heat so that the milk is simmering.
  3. Cook the steak for about 5 minutes on on side, stirring the top occasionally to stop a skin forming on the milk. Then turn the steak over and cook for another 5 minutes on the other side.
  4. Check how well cooked your steak is. If cooked to your liking, serve with a side of jelly beans.
